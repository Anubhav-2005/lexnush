Privacy

A clear account of the data we collect.

LexNush collects only the information needed to respond to inquiries and manage confirmed newsletter subscriptions.

Contact inquiries

When you submit the contact form, we store your name, email address, topic, message, and submission time. We use this information only to review and respond to your inquiry. It is not sold.

Newsletter subscriptions

Newsletter addresses remain pending until you confirm through an email link. You can unsubscribe from future messages using an unsubscribe link in a mailing.

Retention and access

Operational data is retained only for the configured retention period and is accessible only to authorised LexNush staff. To ask for access or deletion, contact editor@lexnush.com.

Security

We use HTTPS in production, protected staff access, server-side validation, CSRF protection, rate limits, and restricted data access. No internet service can promise absolute security; please avoid submitting sensitive case material through this public form.
